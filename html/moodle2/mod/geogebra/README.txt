GeoGebra [1] is a free and multi-platform dynamic mathematics software 
for all levels of education that joins geometry, algebra, tables, graphing, 
statistics and calculus in one easy-to-use package.

This module allows the incorporation of GeoGebra activities in Moodle. Its main 
features are:
- Allows embedding easily GeoGebra activities in some Moodle course.
- Facilitates students tracing because it stores the score, date, duration and 
construction of each of the attempts made ​​by the users.
- Students can save the state of the activities to continue them later.
